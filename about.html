About Teen Tech Support

Who are we? We are teens that excel in using technology and want to help others to become more tech-savvy. We provide a necessary service for people around the United States that need help with technology.

To put it simply, we put all our experience to work for you.












Nila Kumar

Founder and CEO

Nila Kumar, founder of Teen Tech Support, created this company when she was just 14 years old. As a young girl, she got interested in technology and how it works. As she grew up, she learned the inner working of modern technology.

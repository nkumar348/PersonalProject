What do you get from services with Teen Tech Support?

We work with phones, laptops, tablets, printers, and more. We help people in-person (only in the Nebraska area) and online all over the United States.







Pricing

We charge by the hour. If we are unable to fix your issues, you will not be charged for the time spent to fix that problem.

Seniors (age 60+) -- free of cost

Non-seniors -- $30/hour
